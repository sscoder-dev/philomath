As we know that Teachers Day is on September 5 as a birthday of Sarvapalli Radhakrishna. People are talking more and more about education today. Whether you hear something on the news, hear about some college scandal, or discuss the rise of college debt, we all know that change needs to happen in the education industry and without teachers its impossible.

About This Article:- I have written this Article so as to introduce the unknown teachers in world.... So lets get started,

1)William Holmes McGuffey
McGuffey was a college professor who became famous for creating the McGuffey Readers. The Readers were essentially the first elementary-level textbooks and sold over 120 million copies between 1836 and 1960.

2)Toru Kumon
Kumon was a Japanese Math teacher who started creating worksheets to help his son improve his Math skills, which developed into the Kumon method of learning. He started opening learning centres in Japan in the '50s and now they're located throughout the world. The program helps students with Math and language by providing worksheets specially suited to their abilities.

3)Emma Willard
Willard was at the forefront of the women's rights movement and used education to further the cause. She opened the Troy Female Seminary in 1821, which was the first school for women's higher education.

4)Booker T. Washington
Washington was a former slave who put himself through school and then devoted his time to educating children and adults in West Virginia. He also returned to teach at his alma mater (Hampton Institute) and eventually started the Tuskegee Normal and Industrial Institute as well as the National Negro Business League.
